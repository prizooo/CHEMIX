About Our School

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Master Instructor SSEKABIRA CHRISTOPHER, CHEMIX SCHOOL OF BARISTAS AND MIXOLOGISTS has been training professionals in the art of beverage preparation in Muscat, Oman.

We offer both on-campus and online classes to accommodate all students.

Requirements

  • Copy of National ID
  • 4 OMR for school T-shirt
  • 10 OMR certificate fee (paid upon completion)

Rules & Regulations

  1. Chemix school offers 4 courses and each course has its own tuition and duration
  2. All payments must be made at the academy in form of cash or bank transfer
  3. If offered accommodation, follow the rules and regulations of the premises
  4. If caught in any theft, legal action shall be taken

Learn From Master Instructor

SSEKABIRA CHRISTOPHER

With over 12 years of international experience in the beverage industry, SSEKABIRA CHRISTOPHER brings world-class expertise to CHEMIX School. Having worked in premium hotels and cocktail bars across Europe and the Middle East, he founded CHEMIX School in 2020 to share his passion for mixology and barista arts.

Christopher's teaching philosophy focuses on:

  • Hands-on practical training with professional equipment
  • Developing creativity alongside technical skills
  • Understanding the science behind great beverages
  • Building confidence for real-world bar environments

"My greatest joy is seeing students discover their potential and launch successful careers in this exciting industry."
